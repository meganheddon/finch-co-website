What Finch does

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

We made a deliberate choice to build only what small businesses actually asked for. No feature bloat, no enterprise nonsense.

✉️

Beautiful email campaigns

Drag-and-drop editor with templates designed to look good on every inbox. Your emails will look like they came from a studio — not a spreadsheet.

👥

Smart audience tools

Build your list, segment by behaviour, and send the right email to the right person. Tags, groups, and filters that actually make sense.

⚡

Set-and-forget automations

Welcome sequences, birthday emails, abandoned cart reminders. Set them up once and let them run. We handle the timing.

📊

Analytics you can read

Open rates, click rates, revenue from email — presented plainly. No data science degree required. Know what's working in under a minute.

📝

Sign-up forms & landing pages

Build forms that match your brand and embed them anywhere — your website, your Instagram bio, your checkout page. Growing your list shouldn't be a project.

🔗

Works with your stack

Shopify, Squarespace, WooCommerce, and 40+ other integrations. Your data lives where it should, and Finch pulls it in automatically.

Getting started

Up and running in 20 minutes

We've had customers send their first campaign on the same day they signed up. Here's how.

1

Import your list

Upload a CSV, connect your existing tools, or start from scratch with a sign-up form. We'll clean up duplicates and handle the technical bits.

2

Design your email

Pick a template, add your logo and brand colours, drop in your content. The editor is drag-and-drop — no code, no complexity.

3

Send (or schedule it)

Hit send, or pick a time when your audience is most likely to open. We'll let you know how it performed as the data comes in.
